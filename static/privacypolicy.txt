Cleanplayer privacy policy:
- Your data stays on your device at all times and is not sent anywhere
- Your data is used only to communicate with Spotify's API
- E-mail access is only used for Spotify playback SDK
- Logging out (by going into settings) will delete your data from your device
- We use Cloudflare web analytics, but they do not contain personal data (as per their documentation: https://developers.cloudflare.com/analytics/web-analytics)
- Please note that this is a community project made by people that care, and we don't *want* to handle your data
